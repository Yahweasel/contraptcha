What is this???

This is Contraptcha, the word game no one asked for!

(WARNING: These images are AI generated. Some may be racist, erotic, troubling, or anything else.)

Contraptcha is a word puzzle inspired by AI-generated art and CAPTCHAs. In this game, you're presented with four images, each drawn by AI from the same prompt. The prompt is simply a sequence of English nouns. Every time you find one of the words, that's removed from the prompt, so you see the images generated with one word less.

Your goal is, of course, to find every word. If you enter a word that's not part of the prompt, a “semantic similarity” will be shown for whichever word it's most similar to. The semantic similarity is simply a number describing the similarity in concept between the word you entered and the word that's in the prompt. Higher is more similar, and the range is from 0–99, though it's quite rare to go above 70. For instance, if the word is “space” and you enter “stars”, you'll get a high semantic similarity, whereas if the word is “pig” and you enter “stars”, you'll get a low semantic similarity.

If you're stumped and want more hints, you have two options available: (1) You can show how the images would be generated without any of the words you don't know by clicking the word's guess box (the question marks), or by entering a number from 1–6. Remember, what you're doing is removing a word, so if you remove word 4 and the stars disappear from the image, that means word 4 probably is star or stars! (2) You can let the game guess a random word for you by clicking the “hint” button or typing “/hint”.

Remember: AI is stupid and bad at everything, so you may have to think outside the box.

There's currently no scoring system, but basically, as few guesses as possible is what you want, right? Both of the hint systems can probably be considered cheats, and when/if a scoring system is made, using them will count against you.
